# Workforce Security Training Procedures

## Audience
All workforce members with access to Session Co-Pilot PHI.

## Required Training Topics
- HIPAA privacy and security basics
- Role-based access expectations
- Incident reporting and escalation
- Safe credential handling and phishing awareness
- Device security requirements

## Frequency
- Onboarding: before production access
- Annual refresher: every 12 months
- Ad hoc: after major policy or system changes

## Completion Records
Track for each user:
- Full name
- Role
- Date completed
- Instructor or training source
- Assessment result

## Enforcement
Access may be suspended for overdue training.
